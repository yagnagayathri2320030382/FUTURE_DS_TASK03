# Marketing Funnel & Lead Conversion Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the end-to-end marketing funnel to identify conversion bottlenecks, evaluate campaign performance, and recommend strategies to improve lead generation and customer acquisition.

## Objectives

- Analyze visitor-to-lead and lead-to-customer conversions
- Identify drop-off points in the marketing funnel
- Compare marketing channels and campaign performance
- Measure ROI, CAC, and CPL
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## Dataset

A marketing funnel dataset containing visitor, lead, campaign, channel, revenue, and marketing cost information.

## Key Findings

- Organic Search drives the highest traffic but needs better landing page optimization.
- Email Marketing produces the highest-quality leads and customer conversion.
- Referral traffic delivers strong conversion efficiency.
- The largest funnel drop-off occurs between Lead and Marketing Qualified Lead.
- Mobile conversion rates are lower than desktop conversion rates.

## Business Recommendations

- Improve landing page design and calls-to-action.
- Expand investment in Email Marketing and Referral campaigns.
- Optimize Paid Search campaigns for higher ROI.
- Introduce lead scoring and automated nurturing workflows.
- Improve mobile website performance.
- Perform ongoing A/B testing of landing pages and forms.
